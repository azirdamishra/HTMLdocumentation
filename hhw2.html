Colours are present everywhere we look. Our subconscious is affected by colors around us all the time.

Different colors affect us differently.

Color Name Color Significance
Red   energy, passion, action, ambition and determination. It is also the color of anger.
Yellow   mind and the intellect. It is optimistic and cheerful. However it can also suggest impatience, criticism and cowardice.
Orange   social communication and optimism. From a negative color meaning it is also a sign of pessimism and superficiality.
Green   balance and growth. It can mean both self-reliance as a positive and possessiveness as a negative
Blue   trust and peace. It can suggest loyalty and integrity as well as conservatism and frigidity.
Indigo   color of intuition. In the meaning of colors it can mean idealism and structure as well as ritualistic and addictive.
Purple   imagination. It can be creative and individual or immature and impractical.
Turquoise   communication and clarity of mind. It can also be impractical and idealistic.
Pink   unconditional love and nurturing. Pink can also be immature, silly and girlish.
Magenta   universal harmony and emotional balance. It is spiritual yet practical, encouraging common sense and a balanced outlook on life.
Brown   friendly yet serious, down-to-earth color that relates to security, protection, comfort and material wealth.
Gray   ompromise - being neither black nor white, it is the transition between two non-colors. It is unemotional and detached and can be indecisive.
Silver   feminine energy; it is related to the moon and the ebb and flow of the tides - it is fluid, emotional, sensitive and mysterious.
Gold   success, achievement and triumph. Associated with abundance and prosperity, luxury and quality, prestige and sophistication, value and elegance, the color psychology of gold implies affluence, material wealth and extravagance.
White   most complete and pure, the color of perfection. The color meaning of white is purity, innocence, wholeness and completion.
Black   hidden, the secretive and the unknown, creating an air of mystery. It keeps things bottled up inside, hidden from the world.
